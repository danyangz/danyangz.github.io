Biography

I am a Ph.D student in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Washington - Seattle advised by Prof. Tom Anderson and Prof. Arvind Krishnamurthy. Before joining UW, I got my bachelor's degree from University of Illinois - Urbana Champaign advised by Prof. Nitin Vaidya.

Research Interests

I am interested in building systems on strong theoretical foundations. Currently, I am working on datacenter networks and fault-tolerant distributed systems.
