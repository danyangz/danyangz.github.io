Biography

I am a Ph.D student in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Washington - Seattle advised by Tom Anderson and Arvind Krishnamurthy. At different stages of my graduate study, I receive valuable mentorship from Monia Ghobadi, Vincent Liu, Ratul Mahajan and Dan Ports. Before graduate school, I got my bachelor's degree from University of Illinois - Urbana Champaign advised by Nitin Vaidya.

Research Interests

I am interested in building systems on strong theoretical foundations. Currently, I am working on network management and fault-tolerant distributed systems.
